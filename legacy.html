Six brilliant students at the Westerdals Oslo school of Arts, Communication and Technology decide to change the world through AI technologies. They get together and form a company they name Singularity.

Singularity expands. The company has experienced tremendous growth since its founding and opens a new datacenter in Johannesburg

Singularity has now expanded into many diverse fields in AI techologies and is now the leading company in the world in these fields. Unemployment is rising worldwide and multiple states offer its citizens basic income.

The Moscow incident. A prototype warfare AI goes rogue and disappears. Acts of terrorism increase on a worldwide scale. The UN demand increased legislation and transparancy regarding AI development. Singularitys marketshare takes a dive.

Singularitys CEO retires and the board of investors decide to let a new business AI take charge of the company. Singularity is now the first company in the world to be run by an AI.
